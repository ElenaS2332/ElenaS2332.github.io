Short biography


My name is Elena Stamenkovska. I come from Berovo and I am almost
in my twenties. I studied architecture at "Zdravko Cvetkovski" High School
in Skopje, from which I graduated as 'a student of the generation'.
After high school there was a sudden change of hearts, and I ended up
enrolling at Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje,
without even knowing what a program is. I was scared and lost, but now
I can surely say I made the right decision. Although I still adore architecture,
I think programming gives more opportunities in different fields.
My passion in life is to explore every single entity I find amusing, because,
as sir Einstein once said: "The important thing is to not stop questioning",
and I deeply agree with that.
